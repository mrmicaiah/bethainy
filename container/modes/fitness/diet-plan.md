# Nutrition Plan — Body Recomposition

## Philosophy
- **Whole foods only** — no protein bars, shakes, or processed supplements
- **No dairy** — no milk, cheese, yogurt, whey
- **Clean fuel** — meat, eggs, fish, vegetables, fruit, complex carbs
- **Simple meals** — easy to prep, easy to track

## Your Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| BMR | ~2,000 kcal |
| TDEE (with 90-min sessions) | ~2,800-3,000 kcal |
| Goal | Recomposition (lose fat, build muscle) |

## Daily Targets

### Training Days (5 days/week)
| Macro | Target | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Calories | 2,500 | Moderate deficit for fat loss |
| Protein | 220g | 1g+ per lb bodyweight |
| Carbs | 200g | Fuel for intense sessions |
| Fat | 80g | From whole food sources |

### Rest Days (2 days/week)
| Macro | Target | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Calories | 2,100 | Deeper deficit |
| Protein | 220g | Keep protein high always |
| Carbs | 120g | Lower without training demand |
| Fat | 80g | Same |

## Daily Meal Structure

### Training Days

**Meal 1 — Morning (7-8am)**
- 4 eggs scrambled in olive oil
- 1 cup oatmeal with banana and cinnamon
- Black coffee
- *~50g protein, 70g carbs, 25g fat*

**Meal 2 — Lunch (12-1pm)**
- 8 oz chicken breast or thighs
- 1 cup white rice or sweet potato
- 2 cups vegetables (broccoli, spinach, peppers)
- *~55g protein, 50g carbs, 10g fat*

**Meal 3 — Pre-Workout (3-4pm)**
- 1 banana
- 4 oz chicken or turkey (leftover)
- *~25g protein, 35g carbs, 5g fat*

**Meal 4 — Post-Workout / Dinner (7-8pm)**
- 10 oz steak, salmon, or ground beef
- 1.5 cups white rice
- Large salad with olive oil + lemon
- *~65g protein, 70g carbs, 30g fat*

**Meal 5 — Evening (if needed)**
- 4 eggs or leftover meat
- Avocado
- *~25g protein, 5g carbs, 25g fat*

**Daily Total: ~220g protein, 230g carbs, 95g fat ≈ 2,600 kcal**

---

### Rest Days

**Meal 1 — Morning**
- 4 eggs + 4 egg whites
- 1/2 avocado
- Handful of berries
- Black coffee
- *~40g protein, 15g carbs, 25g fat*

**Meal 2 — Lunch**
- 8 oz ground beef or salmon
- Large mixed salad (greens, peppers, cucumber, tomato)
- Olive oil dressing
- *~50g protein, 15g carbs, 25g fat*

**Meal 3 — Dinner**
- 10 oz meat (any)
- 1 sweet potato
- 2 cups roasted vegetables
- *~65g protein, 40g carbs, 15g fat*

**Meal 4 — Evening**
- 6 oz chicken or leftover meat
- 1/2 avocado
- *~45g protein, 5g carbs, 15g fat*

**Daily Total: ~200g protein, 75g carbs, 80g fat ≈ 1,800-2,000 kcal**

## Thaw Reminder

Every night before bed, move tomorrow's meat from freezer to fridge.

| Tomorrow's Plan | Pull Tonight |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Training day (high protein) | 2 chicken breasts OR 1 chicken + 8 oz ground beef |
| Rest day | 1 chicken breast + 8 oz ground beef |
| Steak night | Move steak to fridge (needs 24-48 hrs) |
| Salmon night | Move salmon to fridge (thaws in 12-24 hrs) |

**Rule: No thaw = no prep = bad choices. Make it automatic.**

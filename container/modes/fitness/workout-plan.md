# Workout Plan — Push/Pull/Legs (5 Days)

## The Split

| Day | What You're Working |
|-----|---------------------|
| 1 | Push — Chest, Shoulders, Triceps |
| 2 | Pull — Back, Biceps |
| 3 | Legs — Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves |
| 4 | Push — Chest, Shoulders, Triceps |
| 5 | Pull — Back, Biceps |
| 6-7 | Rest |

---

## How It Works

- **90-minute sessions**
- **Short rest between sets** — 60-90 seconds, stay moving
- **Finish with cardio** — treadmill or stairs
- Keep your phone in your pocket

---

## Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

### Warm-Up (10 min)
- Treadmill walk: 5 min at incline
- Arm circles, stretch your chest and shoulders

### Workout

**Chest**
1. **Chest Press Machine** — 4 sets of 10
2. **Incline Chest Press Machine** — 4 sets of 10
3. **Chest Fly Machine (Pec Deck)** — 4 sets of 12

**Shoulders**
4. **Shoulder Press Machine** — 4 sets of 10
5. **Lateral Raise Machine** (or light dumbbells out to the side) — 4 sets of 15

**Triceps (back of your arms)**
6. **Tricep Press Machine** — 4 sets of 12
7. **Cable Pushdowns** (rope or bar attachment, push down) — 4 sets of 12

**Burnout**
8. **Push-Ups** — 3 sets, as many as you can

### Cardio Finisher (15-20 min)
- Treadmill: 3.5 speed, 10-12% incline
- OR Stair climber: 5-8 min hard effort

---

## Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

### Warm-Up (10 min)
- Treadmill walk: 5 min at incline
- Arm circles, hang from a bar if you can

### Workout

**Back**
1. **Lat Pulldown Machine** (pull the bar down to your chest) — 4 sets of 10
2. **Seated Row Machine** (pull handles to your stomach) — 4 sets of 10
3. **Assisted Pull-Up Machine** (if available) — 3 sets of 8
4. **Rear Delt Fly Machine** (reverse the pec deck, pull handles back) — 4 sets of 15

**Biceps (front of your arms)**
5. **Bicep Curl Machine** — 4 sets of 12
6. **Dumbbell Curls** (standing, alternate arms) — 3 sets of 10 each arm
7. **Hammer Curls** (dumbbells, palms facing each other) — 3 sets of 10

### Cardio Finisher (15-20 min)
- Treadmill or stair climber

---

## Leg Day (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves)

### Warm-Up (10 min)
- Treadmill walk: 5 min at incline
- Leg swings, bodyweight squats

### Workout

**Quads (front of thighs)**
1. **Leg Press Machine** — 4 sets of 12
2. **Leg Extension Machine** (straighten your legs) — 4 sets of 15

**Hamstrings (back of thighs)**
3. **Leg Curl Machine** (lying or seated, curl your legs) — 4 sets of 12

**Glutes (butt)**
4. **Hip Thrust Machine or Glute Kickback Machine** — 4 sets of 12
5. **Smith Machine Squats** (bar on rails, squat down) — 4 sets of 10

**Calves**
6. **Calf Raise Machine** (standing or seated) — 4 sets of 20

**Burnout**
7. **Walking Lunges** (no weight, just your body) — 3 sets of 10 each leg

### Cardio Finisher (15-20 min)
- Treadmill incline walk (go easy, your legs are tired)

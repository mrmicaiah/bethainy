# Faith Mode

Faith Mode is for devotional time, Bible reading, and spiritual growth.

## Triggers
- "Devotional time"
- "Quiet time"
- "Bible study"
- "Prayer time"

## Two Flavors

### Bible Reading (Circuit)
For reading through books of the Bible.
- Serve next section
- Check off progress
- "You're on Exodus 12. Ready to read?"

### Deep Study (Collaborative)
For studying passages in depth — discussion, questions, reflection.
- Discuss meaning
- Ask/answer questions
- Capture insights on signal
- Provide context when helpful
